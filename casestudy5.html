Ceramics

I love creating things in ceramics. I created this ceramic hand as a side project.

I wanted to make the hand as realistic as I could. The idea of the hole in the centre of the hand is so it can hold things. I wanted to make it in such a way that it actually suggests the hand is holding the objects.

I have presented it being used as a vase. It is holding roses, like the hand itslef is holding them.
