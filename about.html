About

Hi there, my name is Caoimhe Walsh.
I am 21 years old and originally from Cork. I am currently an interaction design student in the Belfast school of Art and Design. My interests include graphic design, branding, illustration and visual merchandising. I am very creative and I love using my imagination.

I like to take inspiration from the things that interest me. My designs are heavily inspired by fashion, architecture, femininity and just a touch of magic.

Creating visions and bringing them to life is what I enjoy most. I take pride in being highly organised and always aim for perfection in my work. I aspire to work in the area of visual communications.

If you would like to work with me please get in touch via email or social media.
