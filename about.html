About

Hi there my name is Caoimhe Walsh.
I am 21 years old and originally from Cork. I am currently an interaction design student in the Belfast school of art and design. My interest are graphic design, branding, illustration and visual merchandising. I love being creative and my imagination is one of my favourite tools.

I like to take inspiration from the things that interest me. My designs are heavily inspired by fashion, architecture, femininity, and an air of mystery and magic.

Creating visions and bringing them to life is what I enjoy most. I aspire to work in the area of visual communications, in a fashion related sector.
