Brief

We were given the task to design 12 icons, all in a set. It could be any topic of choice

I wanted it to be as creative as possible. With this task, I really wanted to challenge my imagination

Process

Firstly, I began to brainstorm my ideas. After multiple mind maps and sketches I had narrowed my ideas down to bottles. I thought it would be a challenge to try and illustrate glass so I wanted to work around this idea.

As I love detail I really wanted to focus my project on that. I began to think and sketch out different bottle shapes taking inspiration from perfume bottles. The more I sketched, the more I thought about the idea of potion bottles. This seemed perfect as I could be as creative as I wanted with my designs.

Icons

The icon creation began. I was really excited about this project as I could be as imaginative as I wanted to be. I decided to come up with all the potion types first which was really fun. I then based the designs of the bottles off the purpose of the potions

The shapes of each potion bottle are meant to mirror the potion itself. Whether it be softer or harder edges or tall or small, I have tried to mirror the intent of the spell.

The elements on the bottles are either direct symbols of the potions themselves or inspired by the theme

Site

Lastly, I created a site to showcase my finished designs. I decided to stick to a very simple layout as I wanted to make sure the focus would be on the illustrations.

The content on the website simply explains the effect of the potion. It gives a list of the different ingredients contained.

I also included buttons to download the icons.
