Brief

We were given the task to design an app under the topic of travel. We had to focus on making the app illustrative.

I wanted to focus it on something I would be interested in using, and what I felt there was not an app already for. This is where th idea for 'QUIRKEY' came about.

Process

I first began to brainstorm. Mind mapping helped me to make decisions and explore my ideas. I was thinking about travel across cities all over the world and, how hard to can be to find amazing bespoke, one off boutiques. Wouldn't it be so useful if there was something there to help you find these stores?

I next started to sketch out my ideas. I created early versions of wireframes to help me decide on a layout. I then began to think of colours and concepts. I created the icons for my app also. I wanted to focus on the visual aspect as much as possible.

Solution

I decided to design an app to solve this. It's purpose is to locate bespoke boutiques in cities around the world. You simply choose the country you are travelling to, select the city and then the kind of store you are looking for. You will then be presented with an illustration of the city, showing location points of the relevant stores. When you click on the store, the location, a brief description and contact details will appear. Lastly you can use you own location to see how close you are.

The design of the app is meant to exude dreaminess through the colour scheme and illustration style. The thought behind this is to enhance the idea of traveling away to knew cities and unknown places to discover - just like in your dreams. The pastels create a soft aesthetic the feminine vibes.
